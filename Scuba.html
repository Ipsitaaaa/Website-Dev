SCUBA DIVING IN ANDAMAN

Are you ready to dive deeper?

Dive into the turquoise and deep blue waters of the Andaman and Nicobar Islands.
Scuba Diving is in every bollywood fans wishlist after watching Zindagi Na Milegi Dobara.
So? Are you all set to explore the deep hidden treasures of the Ocean. Now here is your chance!

Andaman is said to be the best place for Scuba Diving in India. It offers a vibrantand breathtaking marine life with pristine coral reefs.
It is overwhelming to explore the underwater life so closely just like we see in Discovery and Natgeowild.
Andaman has a historical significance in Indian History. In Cellular Jail, the Britishers used to imprison the freedom fighters of India and torture them inhumanly and later dispose their corpse into the Bay Of Bengal.
The Andaman and Nicobar Islands have a tropical rainforest canopy, made of a mixed flora with elements from Indian, Myanmar, Malaysian and endemic floral strains.
This tropical rain forest, despite its isolation from adjacent landmasses, is surprisingly rich with a diversity of animal life.About 50 varieties of forest mammals are found to occur in the Andaman and Nicobar Islands. Some are endemic, including the Andaman wild boar.

Diving Info

  • Region: South India

  • Max Depth:13-28 mts(varies according to experience)

  • Duration:30 mins

  • Grade: Easy to Moderate

  • Suitable For: 10-55 years

  • Experience: Beginners + Experienced

  • Best Season: October-May

  • Location: Elephant Beach,Havelock island

CAUTION

  • Patients with Heart Problem, High blood pressure, Asthma, Epilepsy are not fit for these missions.

  • Avoid consuming heavy food or alcoholic beverages before and after the activity.

  • This activity is not recommended for women who are more than 3months pregnant, anyone that has had major surgery, or has a broken leg/arm, has chronic back or neck pains, etc.

  • Carry Extra pair of clothes.Carrying sharp objects, lighter, knife etc is strictly prohibited.

  • Not suitable for people who have undergone recent surgery
